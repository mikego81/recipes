Ingredients:
- 1/2 cup Heavy Whipping Cream
- 1/4 cup sugar
- 1 tsp mexican vanilla
- 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
- 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Directions:
1. Beat the heavy whipping cream on high until stiff peaks
2. Add the sugar, vanilla, nutmeg and cinnamon
3. Serve, enjoy, with a cup-o-joe preferably!!!
